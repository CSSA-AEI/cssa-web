Tired of making and joining several new chats or Discords every semester? Are you looking for the same sense of pre-pandemic community? Join our Official EECS Discord server! The EECS Discord acts as a central hub for engineering students to talk about classes, study, or simply chill out and socialize. Earn roles, make new friends, and most of all, you never have to worry about missing out on any messages.

Fatigué de faire et de rejoindre plusieurs nouveaux chats ou Discords chaque semestre ? Vous cherchez à retrouver le même sentiment de communauté pré-pandémique ?
